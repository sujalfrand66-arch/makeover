About Glamour

Home › About

Est. 2019

Born From a Passion
For Beauty & Care

Glamour Beauty Parlour was founded in 2019 with a singular dream: to bring luxury beauty experiences to the women of Suratgarh. Located in the heart of Swami Vivekananda Nagar, we have quickly become the city's most trusted destination for premium beauty services.

Our journey began with a commitment to excellence — using only the finest products, hiring the most skilled artists, and creating an environment where every client feels like royalty. Today, with a perfect 5-star rating and hundreds of happy clients, we continue to set the standard for beauty in Suratgarh.

Whether you're preparing for your wedding day, a special occasion, or simply treating yourself — Glamour Beauty Parlour is your sanctuary of style and self-care.
