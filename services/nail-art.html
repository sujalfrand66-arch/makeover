Nail Art

Express your personality through stunning nail art. Our creative nail specialists are trained in a wide range of nail art techniques — from classic French tips and gel polish to intricate hand-painted designs, ombre nails, and 3D embellishments.

Whether you want minimal, elegant nails for everyday wear or dramatic, show-stopping designs for a special occasion, our team will craft nails that perfectly complement your look.

We use premium gel polishes and nail care products that are gentle on your nails, chip-resistant, and long-lasting. Your nails are in expert, caring hands at Glamour Beauty Parlour.

Key Benefits

✦Long-lasting gel & acrylic options
✦Wide range of nail art designs
✦Safe, non-toxic nail products
✦Custom designs for weddings & events
✦Manicure & pedicure packages
✦Quick-dry gel finishes available
